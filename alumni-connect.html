Alumni Connect Platform

Summary: Alumni-student networking platform enabling mentorship, referrals, and meaningful professional connections.

Purpose: Bridge students with alumni for guidance, internships, jobs, and collaborative opportunities.

Stack: MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), JWT Auth, REST APIs.

Contributions & Impact: Designed referral/mentorship flows, implemented role-based access, improved engagement metrics; reduced time-to-connect by streamlining invites and matching.
